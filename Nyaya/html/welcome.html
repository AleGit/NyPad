Welcome to Nyāya

Nyāya is an interactive environment to learn simple facts about the formalism of propositional logic and standard transformations of Boolean functions. It is integrated with BoolTool of the Department of Compututational Logic at the Institute of Computer Science on the University of Innsbruck) which allows the manipulation and evaluation of boolean functions. BoolTool supports different representations of boolean functions and a variety of different algorithms.
